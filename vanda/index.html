I’d love to show you a few examples of projects that I have worked on.

Penguin Monarchs

An ambitious series covering every monarch from Athelstan to Elizabeth II, with covers that connect to form a royal family tree.

A deceptively simple design; each royal house is colour coded, and when complete the full family tree will measure five metres from top to bottom. I negotiated a very low retail price for the books, working closely with colleagues in Production, and external printers, to identify the best possible finishes within an incredibly tight budget.

Pelican Books

Overseeing the relaunch of a celebrated non-fiction imprint, making it fit for a new generation.

I have art directed every aspect of the Pelican imprint since 2014, leading a tender process and working with internal and external colleagues across editorial, technology, marketing and production to develop a consistent and distinctive visual language that can be applied across a wide variety of books and formats.
