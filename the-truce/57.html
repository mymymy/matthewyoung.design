Thursday, May 9th

I can’t talk to her in the office. It has to be somewhere else. I’m studying her schedule. She often prefers to eat downtown. She has lunch with a friend, a fat woman who works for the London Paris department store. But afterwards, they part company and she goes to a café at 25th and Misiones for a drink. It has to be a chance encounter. It’s the best way.
