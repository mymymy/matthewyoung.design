Oh, hello. I'm Matthew Young.
I live in London.

By day I work for a certain sphenisciform publisher, designing book covers for our non-fiction and classics titles.

And in my spare time I make animations, illustrations, websites, and anything else that tickles my fancy. Recent clients have included the Royal Society of Arts, the British Film Institute, World Book Day and the National Portrait Gallery.
