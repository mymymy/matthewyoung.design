The brief was simply to make Elmwood remember my name. Unfortunately, there were also 87 other students trying to make Elmwood remember their names.

I made this video, and presented it to Ben Greengrass, Creative Director at Elmwood.

It did the trick, and I won a work placement there. Back of the net.

Having won the work placement with Elmwood, I had a few questions I wanted to ask them before I began.

Instead of putting my questions in a boring email, I thought it would be a tad more exciting to animate them instead.
