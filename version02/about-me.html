ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm Matthew Young - a one man design machine, passionate about graphic design, animation, illustration and typography, amongst other things.

I graduated from the University of Leeds (with a First) in 2010, and was named 'Graduate of the Year' by Creative Review. Since finishing uni I've enjoyed a six month internship with Brass, and as of February 2011, I landed my dream job designing book covers for Penguin.
